ClusterValidityIndices.jl

These pages serve as the official documentation for the ClusterValidityIndices.jl Julia package.

Cluster Validity Indices (CVI) tackle the problem of judging the performance of an unsupervised/clustering algorithm without the availability of truth or supervisory labels, resulting in metrics of under- or over-partitioning. Furthermore, Incremental CVIs (ICVI) are variants of these ordinarily batch algorithms that enable an online and computationally tractable method of evaluating the performance of a clustering algorithm as it clusters while being numerically equivalent to their batch counterparts.

The purpose of this package is to provide a home for the development and use of these CVIs and ICVIs.

See the Index for the complete list of documented functions and types.
